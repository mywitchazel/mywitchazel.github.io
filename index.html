ABOUT US

Focusing On What Matters Most

Our story begins with a native North American botanical that has soothed skin for centuries —witch hazel, the gentle yet powerful shrub known as Hamamelis virginiana. Long before it became a modern skincare essential, witch hazel was carefully harvested and prepared by Indigenous tribes who understood its natural ability to calm, cleanse, and restore. Their traditional knowledge revealed the plant’s remarkable gifts: cooling inflamed skin, reducing swelling, easing irritation, and bringing balance back to the body.

Inspired by this deep heritage, we honor the wisdom of the land and the people who first nurtured it. We source our witch hazel with intention, using mindful extraction methods that preserve the plant’s natural integrity. Every formula begins with pure botanical clarity — clean, alcohol-free distillations that capture witch hazel’s true essence without stripping or harshness.

From forest to bottle, our products stay rooted in simplicity, purity, and respect. No unnecessary additives. No overpowering scents. Just the timeless clarity of a plant that has supported skin wellness for generations.

We believe in skincare that feels connected — to nature, to history, and to the quiet rituals that ground us. Through witch hazel’s soothing touch, we carry forward a tradition of healing and harmony, offering modern beauty that remembers where it comes from.

Nature’s heritage. Your skin’s sanctuary.

HISTORY

Indigenous Origins

Tribes such as the Cherokee, Iroquois, and Potawatomi used witch hazel extracts for swelling, skin healing, and cleansing.

1800s
European settlers adopt witch hazel after learning its uses from Native American healers. Distillation methods begin.

Mid-1800s
Commercial witch hazel tonics appear in the U.S.

1900s
Witch hazel becomes a household staple for first aid and skincare.

Today
Witch hazel is now a global skincare staple, celebrated for its gentle, astringent, and healing properties. Modern formulas honor its traditional uses while providing safe, and effective solutions for all skin types.
